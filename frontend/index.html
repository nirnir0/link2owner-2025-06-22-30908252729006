    • Frequently Asked Questions

    How human-like does the AI sound?
    Our AI uses advanced voice synthesis with natural pauses, emotional inflection, and perfect diction. In blind tests, 92% of listeners couldn't distinguish it from a human agent. The AI adapts its tone to be friendly, professional, or urgent based on conversation context.
    Can it handle complex negotiations?
    Absolutely. Our AI is trained on thousands of real estate negotiations and can handle price discussions, terms adjustments, and closing strategies. For extremely complex cases, it can seamlessly transfer the conversation to a human agent while providing full context.
    How does the AI upsell additional services?
    The AI identifies opportunities during conversations to naturally introduce our partner services. For example, when a client mentions budget concerns, it might suggest our loan partnerships. When discussing property condition, it introduces interior services. All recommendations are contextually relevant and delivered conversationally.
    What's the setup process?
    Implementation takes 3 simple steps: 1) We integrate with your existing CRM or lead sources, 2) Customize the AI's knowledge base with your properties and services, 3) Train your team on the orchestration dashboard. Most clients are fully operational within 48 hours.
